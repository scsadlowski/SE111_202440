About Hackers

Rating: PG-13

Run Time: 1h 45m

Release Date:Sept. 15, 1995.

Genres: Cyber Thriller Crime Drama Romance Thriller

Stars: Jonny Lee Miller  Angelina Jolie  Jesse Bradford

This is a film released in the mid 90's, classified as an American Cyber Crime Thriller, directed by Iain Softley and stars Jonny Lee Miller, Angelina Jolie, Jesse Bradford, Matt Lillard, Laurence Mason, Renoly Santiago, Lorraine Bracco, and Fisher Stevens. The movie features Dade Murphy as a genius child who took out Wall Street and is banned from computers until adulthood. Now as an adult Dade finds himself and his high school friends in some trouble being framed in a cyber theft.
